Sourdough Bread

The most delicious buns you have ever made

A simple recipe to make the most stunning sourdough buns with the perfect tang.

When baking bread it is important to use your kitchen scale instead of measuring cups as you seem more competent.

Ingredients

  • 200 g (3 dl) whole grain flour
  • 500 g (8 dl) high protein wheat flour
  • 100 g (1.5 dl) sour dough starter
  • 500 g (5 dl) water
  • Optional: seeds as garnish

Steps

  1. Mix flour, water, and sourdough starter in a bowl
  2. Leave to rise overnight
  3. Preheat over to 225 Â°C
  4. Flour a table and transfer dough to the table
  5. Garnish dough with seeds
  6. Cut dough into 12 pieces and place on baking tray.
  7. Bake for 15 minutes
