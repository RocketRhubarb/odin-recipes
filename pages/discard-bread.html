Discard bread

Stop wasting a precious resource!

Do you discard a lot of perfectly good sourdough?

Stop wasting this precious resource by saving the bread in a jar. When you have saved around half a litre (one pint) you are ready to bake this bread.

Ingredients

  • approximately 500g sourdough discard
  • 200g whole grain wheat flour
  • 200g water
  • 100g sunflower or pumpkin seeds
  • 7g salt

Steps

  1. Mix together all ingredients and pour into a non-stick bread pan.
  2. Leave to rise for 8-12 hours
  3. Bake at 250°C for 20 minutes. It is done when the core temperature reaches 95°C.
