Sourdough Starter

What you need to become start your sourdough journey

A starter is the raising agent used in sourdough baking and is used instead of yeast or baking powder.

A simple recipe to make get started making sourdough bread.

Ingredients

  • whole grain flour (preferred to start with)
  • wheat flour
  • water
  • a glass jar

Steps

  1. Day 1-3: Mix 25 g of water with 25 g whole grain flour
  2. Day 1-3: Leave in a warm place for 24 hours with open lid
  3. Repeat step 1 and 2 for three days. After a day or two, you might see some small bubbles.
  4. At day 4-14, discard half your sourdough and add 50 g water and 50 g wheat flower.
  5. Continue feeding the sourdough until it produces large bubbles and smells zingy after a few hours.
    It's ready as soon as it rises by more than 50 %.
